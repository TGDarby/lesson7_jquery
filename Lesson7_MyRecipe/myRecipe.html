Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 14oz can of condensed milk (sweetened)
  • 1 12oz can of evaporated milk
  • 1 tbsp of vanilla extract

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 F.

Melt sugar until liquified and golden in saucepan.

Beat eggs, add condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla until it is smooth.

Pour into baking dish and cover.

Bake for 60 minutes.

Enjoy!
